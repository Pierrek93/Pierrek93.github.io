Robin Williams

The kindest man who made so many laugh.

Here's a time line of Robin Williams's life:

  • 1951 - Born in Chicago, Illinois
  • 1969 - Graduates from highschool and is voted "Funniest" by his classmates
  • 1973 - Williams attained a full scholarship to the Juilliard School in New York City
  • 1976 - Starts performing stand-up comedy in the San Francisco Bay Area
  • 1977 - debut TV appearance on the show "Laugh in"
  • 1979 - Williams wins a Grammy Award for Best Comedy Album for the recording of his live show at the Copacabana in New York, "Reality ... What a Concept".
  • 1986 - Williams teamed up with Whoopi Goldberg and Billy Crystal to found Comic Relief USA. This annual HBO television benefit devoted to the homeless has raised $80 million as of 2014
  • 1989 - Marries Marsha Garces with who he has two children
  • 2003 - Williams performed with the USO for U.S. troops stationed in Iraq and Afghanistan.
  • 2008 - Divorces from Marsha Garces
  • 2014 - dies at the age of 63.

"No matter what people tell you, words and ideas can change the world."

-- Robin Williams --
